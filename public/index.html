About me

I'm an energetic, 1 year old German Shepard/Black Mouth Cur mix looking for my forever home. I love to play and keep active, going on long walks and exploring. My big ears help me pick up all the interesting things I can find on my adventures.

About you

You could be literally anyone!

If you're an active single person, I would be a great companion for you. I can accompany you on hikes, runs, and other adventures. I'm up for anything - especially if it involves lots of playtime and bonding.

Or maybe you're part of a family with other dogs? I would be absolutely delighted! I love to run around with other pooches, explore the outdoors and get plenty of exercise. I'm a great addition to a family and can bring lots of fun and love to your home.

Ears!

One of my best features is my great big ears. They make it look like I'm a flapping flag when I sprint to chase my favorite toy - plus I can HEAR LITERALLY EVERYTHING. I'm sure they will make you smile every time you see them!
